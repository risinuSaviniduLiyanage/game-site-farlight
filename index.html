About The characters

Maggie

Maggie stands out as a tank hero, known for her defensive abilities and capacity to absorb damage for her team..

Maychelle

Maychelle is known for her mobility and agility in the game. She is a popular pick for players who prefer fast-paced, evasive gameplay, often outmaneuvering opponents with her skills..

Freddie

Freddie stands out as a tank/defender hero with a focus on sustainability and damage mitigation, making him a valuable asset for teams that need durability in combat.

Momoi

Momoi is known for her supportive abilities and agility. She brings versatility to the battlefield with her team healing and mobility, making her a valuable asset in any squad.

Phantom

Phantom is known for his ability to vanish from sight and take enemies by surprise, making him a formidable choice for ambushes and flanking maneuvers.

Nightwatch

renowned for his exceptional scouting abilities and tactical playstyle. Nightwatch thrives on gathering information about enemy positions and providing crucial intel to his team, making him a valuable asset for long-range engagements and team coordination.

Beau

known for his unique skill set that combines offensive capabilities with support mechanics. He excels at adapting to various combat situations, making him a well-rounded choice for players looking to balance damage output with team assistance..

Ducksyde

known for his unorthodox style and playful abilities that make him a fun choice for players. His character design and gameplay mechanics contribute to his distinct role on the battlefield..
