About farlight 84 game

Developed by Lilith Games and published by Farlight Games in April 2023, Farlight 84 is a fast-paced cross-platform Hero Battle Royale game. With its unique skills, diverse vehicles, and massive firepower across both land and sky, Farlight 84 delivers quicker battles and compact mayhem, setting it apart from traditional gaming experiences!
